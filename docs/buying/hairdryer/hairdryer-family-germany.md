### 3.4 Key Selection Criteria for Families (Priority Order)

1. **Heat protection technology** — ThermoShield (Philips), SensoDryer (Braun), or brushless motor self-regulation. Most important for children's sensitive scalps (max safe: 48°C for age 2–7; 60°C for age 8–12; never use high heat on children <2) [S16]
2. **Weight** — Target <450g for child safety and wrist strain prevention. Most AC budget models are 600–700g; brushless models can be <350g [S16]
3. **Noise level** — Target ≤70 dB for children. Budget AC models: 75–85 dB. Brushless models: 58–65 dB [S16]
4. **Motor type & durability** — DC motors: 400–700h lifespan. AC motors: ~2000h. Brushless: 10,000h+ [S17]
5. **Variable heat/speed settings** — Minimum: 3 heat + 2 speed + cool-shot. Cool-shot must be a true cold setting (not just reduced heat) [S18]
6. **Attachments** — Concentrator (precision styling) + diffuser (volume/curls) are the minimum. A wide-tooth comb is useful for medium-thick hair [S19]
7. **Wattage** — 2000–2400W for medium-thick hair. Below 1800W will be slow on thick hair [S20]

### 3.5 Technology Reality Check

**Ionic technology:** Effect is real but "generally quite weak" in independent tests. Reduces static; marginally speeds drying. Not a major differentiator — don't pay a premium for it alone [S21].

**Ceramic/tourmaline coating:** Improves heat distribution. Genuine benefit for fine or damaged hair. Less critical for medium-thick hair that can tolerate more heat [S19].

**Oil-infused grilles (macadamia, argan, coconut):** Independent testers call this "Budenzauber" (marketing gimmick). Benefits unmeasurable in practice [S21].

**Motor type:** This is the single most important technical specification. AC > DC for family durability. Brushless > AC for weight, noise, and longevity [S17].

### 3.6 Safe Usage Protocol for Families

- **Towel-dry first** (microfiber if possible); let air-dry 10–15 min before dryer
- **Apply heat protectant** before each session (spray, not cream for medium-thick hair)
- **Keep dryer moving** — never hold in one spot; maintain ≥15 cm from scalp
- **Children under 2:** towel-dry only — no hairdryer
- **Children 2–7:** 38–48°C warm mode only; always adult-supervised; ≥15 cm distance
- **Children 8–12:** up to 60°C; supervised only; independent use from ~12–14
- **Cool-shot to finish** every session — seals cuticle, locks style, safer for kids
- **Maintenance:** clean rear filter monthly; never wrap cord tightly; store cool/dry [S22]
