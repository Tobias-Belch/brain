## Alignment-Friendly Tee Connectors

For the custom brass manifold, the difficult part is connecting tees while keeping all tee branches aligned. A plain threaded sleeve or nipple connects tees mechanically, but it does not solve angular alignment because final rotation is determined by thread-tightening depth.

The target part is a true union / movable screw connection with a union nut and flat gasket. With the updated all-male tee choice (AG/AG/AG), a 1" IG/IG flat-sealing union is the correct tee-to-tee connector and does not need extra nipples.

## Connector Geometry Options

| Connector type | Works between two AG/AG/AG male tees? | Extra nipples needed per union | Verdict |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| IG/IG true union | Yes | 0 | Best geometry for all-male tees |
| IG/AG true union | Yes | 1 female/female sleeve or different tee geometry | Works, but less clean |
| AG/AG true union | No | Requires female adapters | Wrong direction for all-male tees |
| IG/IG plain sleeve | Mechanically yes | 0 | Not alignment-friendly; avoid for tee-to-tee alignment |

## Recommendation

Use 1" IG/IG flat-sealing movable unions directly between the male-threaded tee ends.

For the current 4-tee manifold layout with 3 tee-to-tee connections:

| Part | Qty | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| 1" AG/AG/AG brass tee | 4 | Main manifold body |
| 1" IG/IG flat-sealing movable union | 3 | Alignment-friendly tee-to-tee connector |

This avoids the extra nipples that would be required with all-female tees.

## Tee Part Geometry

The tee geometry can also be varied, but it does not remove the need for alignment-friendly unions. The branch orientation still has to be controlled independently from thread-tightening depth.

The selected tee type is AG/AG/AG: all three ports are male. This pairs directly with the selected IG/IG flat-sealing unions between tees and avoids extra tee-to-tee nipples.

## Tee Geometry Options

| Tee type | Inline connection impact | Branch connection impact | Availability | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| IG/IG/IG | Needs male-ended connector pieces between tees | Accepts common AG ball valves, plugs, nipples, and GEKA adapters | High | Previous fallback |
| AG/AG/AG | Connects directly through IG/IG flat-sealing unions | Branch needs female ball valve/cap/adapter | Lower, but selected | Best for the chosen union |
| AG/IG/AG inline with IG branch | Could pair directly with IG/AG unions in one direction | Branch remains easy if IG | Low / ambiguous listings | Only useful if a verified part is found |
| IG/AG/IG or mixed tee | May save one nipple in some positions but complicates orientation and ordering | Depends on which port is male | Low | Too error-prone for this build |

## Tee Recommendation

Use AG/AG/AG brass tees if the selected all-male tee product clearly confirms all three ports are G1" male threads. This makes the MegaHaustechnik IG/IG flat-sealing union a direct tee-to-tee connector. Avoid mixed-thread tees unless product photos or drawings clearly show which port is male/female.

For the current manifold layout, use:

| Part | Qty | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| Brass T-piece 1" AG/AG/AG | 4 | Main rail body; all inline ports face downward in the mounted layout |
| 1" IG/IG flat-sealing movable union | 3 | Alignment-friendly tee-to-tee joints, direct connection between male tee ends |

This combination keeps all tees identical and avoids separate tee-to-tee nipples.

## Tee Search Terms

Use these first for the selected all-male tee geometry:

- `Messing T-Stück 1 Zoll AG AG AG`
- `Messing T-Stück 1 Zoll Außengewinde`
- `T-Stück G1 Zoll Messing Außengewinde`
- `Gewindefitting T-Stück 1 Zoll AG Messing DN25`
- `T-Stück 90 Grad G1 Außengewinde Messing`

Fallback all-female tee terms:

- `Messing T-Stück 1 Zoll IG IG IG`
- `Messing T-Stück 1 Zoll Innengewinde`
- `T-Stück G1 Zoll Messing Innengewinde`
- `Gewindefitting T-Stück 1 Zoll Messing DN25`
- `T-Stück 90 Grad G1 Innengewinde Messing`

Only use these if deliberately checking mixed-thread tees:

- `Messing T-Stück 1 Zoll AG IG AG`
- `T-Stück 1 Zoll Außengewinde Innengewinde Messing`
- `T-Stück G1 AG IG AG Messing`
- `T-Stück 1 Zoll männlich weiblich Messing`

Avoid relying on mixed-thread tees unless product photos or drawings clearly show which of the three tee ports are male/female.

- `Messing Verschraubung G1 IG IG flachdichtend`
- `bewegliche Verschraubung 1 Zoll IG IG`
- `Durchgangsverschraubung 1 Zoll IG IG flachdichtend`

Fallback IG/AG union terms:

- `Verschraubung 1 Zoll IG AG flachdichtend beweglich`
- `Messing Verschraubung G1 IG AG flachdichtend`
- `bewegliche Verschraubung 1 Zoll IG AG`
- `Durchgangsverschraubung 1 Zoll IG AG flachdichtend`

Also try these if searching for the ideal but harder-to-find geometry:

- `Verschraubung 1 Zoll AG AG flachdichtend beweglich`
- `Messing Verschraubung G1 AG AG flachdichtend`
- `Holländer 1 Zoll AG AG flachdichtend`

Avoid results described primarily as:

- `Doppelnippel`
- `Langnippel`
- `Rohrnippel`
- `conical sealing`
- `konisch dichtend`

These either do not solve alignment or are less suitable than flat-sealing union connections.
