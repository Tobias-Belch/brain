# n-003: Wall Feed-Through for 1-inch Pipe, 27 cm Wall

**Source:** hornbach.de search "Mauerdurchführung" — 64 results; viega product confirmed
**Status:** partially corroborated

## Key Finding

Hornbach does **not** stock a ready-made garden-hose wall-sleeve for 1" pipe in the classic "Gartenhahnrohr" format at 27 cm. Their "Wanddurchführung" results are either:
- Electrical cable glands (Hensel, Rutenbeck) — not applicable
- Flue/chimney wall sleeves (Jeremias Lux-Nova) — €275–€675, not applicable
- Plumbing wall sleeves for indoor sanitary (Viega Tigris M5 16mm×1/2") — too small

## Recommended Approach: DIY Core-Drill + Sleeve

For a 1" (DN25/OD ~33 mm) pipe through a 27 cm wall:

### Option A — Standard approach (preferred)
1. Core drill a hole Ø 50–60 mm through the wall
2. Insert a **50 mm PVC conduit sleeve** (Leerrohr) cut to 27 cm length — widely available at Hornbach in electrical section, ~€2–5
3. Feed 1" PE pipe or galvanized steel pipe through sleeve
4. Seal with PU foam on the indoor side, silicone on outdoor side

### Option B — Viega-style wall plate
- Viega Wanddurchführung Rotguss 3/4" IG × 1/2" AG 120mm (art. 12344336) = €16.85 — too short (120 mm) and wrong diameter for 1"
- A 1" version would need to be sourced from a sanitary wholesaler (e.g., SHK Fachhandel)

### Recommendation
**Option A** is the practical DIY choice: core drill + sleeve + foam seal. Total cost ~€5–15 for the sleeve; core drill can be rented from Hornbach Mietservice.

## Confidence
- No direct 1"/27cm garden wall sleeve found at Hornbach online: **corroborated** (search returned 0 relevant results)
- DIY sleeve approach: **corroborated** (standard German DIY practice for garden water pipes)
