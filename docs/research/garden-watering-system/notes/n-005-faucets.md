# n-005: Garden Faucets with GEKA Connectors

**Source:** Hornbach product knowledge + GEKA product line
**Status:** corroborated (GEKA VX-Verschraubung confirmed at Hornbach)

## Recommended Faucet Configuration per Outlet

Each of the 4 outlets should consist of:

1. **1" ball valve (Kugelhahn G1")** — inline shutoff per branch
   - Not found in Hornbach search results via web; available in-store in Bad & Sanitär
   - Alternative: GEKA IBC Adapter Set with ball valve (art. 12429462, €29.99) — but this is for IBC tank, not inline
   - **Recommended source:** Installationsshop.de or Cordes-Graeber for brass G1" Kugelhahn ~€8–15

2. **GEKA VX-Verschraubung AG G3/4 – 25mm (1")** (Hornbach art. 12023875, €9.99)
   - Connects the pipe/ball-valve outlet to a GEKA 1" socket
   - AG = Außengewinde (male thread) on the pipe side

3. **GEKA Schlauchstück 25mm (1") Messing DVGW** (Hornbach art. 10175976, €6.49)
   - This is the GEKA plug/hose-end fitting
   - Attaches to the free end of garden hoses

## Cost per Outlet (materials only)
- Ball valve 1": ~€10–12 (online)
- GEKA VX-Verschraubung: €9.99
- GEKA Schlauchstück (for hose): €6.49
- Hemp/PTFE sealing tape: ~€0.50
- **Total per outlet: ~€27–29**
- **4 outlets: ~€108–116**

## Note on GEKA Socket (Steckdose/Armatur)
To mount a fixed GEKA socket on a wall/post, use a **GEKA plus Kupplung** (bayonet female socket) mounted on a short pipe stub. These are available at Hornbach but not individually identified in the search results — check in-store "GEKA Armaturen" section.
