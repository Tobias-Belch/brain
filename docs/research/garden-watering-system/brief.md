# Research Brief

## Question
What are the optimal parts, suppliers, and setup design for a garden watering system connected to a TIP GP INOX 5000 pump, maximizing pipe/fitting diameter (1 inch preferred, 3/4 inch fallback), with a wall feed-through (27 cm wall) and 4 GEKA-connector faucets — available in Berlin, Germany or German online shops at best value?

## Intended Use
- Purchase a concrete parts list and go to a store
- Estimate total cost before committing
- Validate the system design before ordering
- Receive a proposal for an optimal setup

## Depth and Checkpoint Mode
- Depth: deep
- Mode: default (checkpoints enabled)

## Scope
- In scope:
  - Pump outlet fittings (1 inch female/male BSP or G1")
  - Wall feed-through sleeve/pipe for 27 cm concrete/brick wall
  - Main line pipe (1 inch, rigid or flexible)
  - Distribution manifold or T/cross fittings to split to 4 outlets
  - Shut-off valves per outlet (optional but advisable)
  - GEKA quick-connect couplings (1 inch or 3/4 inch)
  - Faucets/taps with GEKA or compatible to GEKA
  - Hose connectors downstream of GEKA (out of scope for this design, user-supplied)
  - Suppliers: Berlin brick-and-mortar (Bauhaus, OBI, Hornbach, Hagebau) and German online shops (Amazon.de, bauhaus.de, hagebau.de, gardena.com, toro.com, etc.)
  - Price comparison and value recommendation
- Out of scope:
  - Electrical installation for the pump
  - Underground pipe burial (assumed above-ground or surface-mounted)
  - Drip irrigation sub-systems downstream of the 4 faucets
  - Social media / private sources

## Source Policy
- Preferred source types: web (manufacturer pages, retailer product pages), docs (datasheets)
- Source distrusts / exclusions: none
- Recency constraints: product availability 2024–2026 preferred; avoid discontinued lines

## Tool Policy
- Default allowlist: WebFetch
- Run allow overrides: none
- Run disallow overrides: none

## Evidence Threshold
- Product specs: corroborated (manufacturer + retailer)
- Prices: single-sourced acceptable with recency note
- Compatibility claims: corroborated where possible

## Defaults and Assumptions
- Pump outlet thread: G 1" (1 inch BSP female) — to be confirmed from spec sheet
- Wall material: concrete or brick, 27 cm thick — standard core-drill sleeve assumed
- Pipe type preference: rigid galvanized steel or HDPE for main run; flexible reinforced hose acceptable
- Distribution: 1-to-4 manifold or sequential T-fittings
- GEKA is the de-facto garden quick-connect standard in Germany (DN 25 = 1 inch, DN 20 = 3/4 inch)
- Budget: value-optimized (best quality/price), not cheapest-only
- Installation: DIY
