## 3-Piece Union / Holländer (alignment-friendly connector between tees)

Use these to find a true 3-piece flat-sealing union that allows free rotation before tightening:

- `Messing Verschraubung 1 Zoll IG IG flachdichtend`
- `Verschraubung 1 Zoll IG IG flachdichtend beweglich`
- `bewegliche Verschraubung 1 Zoll IG IG`
- `Durchgangsverschraubung 1 Zoll IG IG flachdichtend`
- `Messing Verschraubung 1 Zoll flachdichtend`
- `Holländer Verschraubung 1 Zoll Messing`
- `bewegliche Verschraubung 1 Zoll`
- `flachdichtende Verschraubung 1 Zoll IG AG`
- `Rohrverschraubung lösbar 1 Zoll Messing`
- `Messing Verschraubung 1 Zoll IG AG flachdichtend Überwurfmutter`
- `Verschraubung 1 Zoll Überwurfmutter flachdichtend Messing`

**What to look for:** 3-piece construction (two threaded halves + union nut / Überwurfmutter), flat gasket (Flachdichtung), free rotation of each half before the nut is tightened. With the selected all-male AG/AG/AG tees, prefer IG/IG unions. Avoid conical sealing (`konisch` / `kegelförmig`) — those lock rotation at final tightening depth.

## Brass T-Piece (tee fitting for the rail body)

- `Messing T-Stück 1 Zoll AG Außengewinde`
- `T-Stück G1 Zoll Messing Außengewinde AG/AG/AG`
- `Gewindefitting T-Stück 1 Zoll AG Messing DN25`
- `Messing T-Stück 1 Zoll IG Innengewinde`
- `T-Stück G1 Zoll Messing Innengewinde IG/IG/IG`
- `Gewindefitting T-Stück 1 Zoll Messing DN25`

**What to look for:** All-male (AG/AG/AG), BSP/G-thread (parallel, not tapered R/NPT), 1 inch, brass. All three tee ports should be male-threaded. Avoid tapered R-thread tees as they don't pair cleanly with flat-sealing unions.

## GEKA Adapter (hose coupling for outlets)

**Fixed (non-rotating):**
- `GEKA Kupplung 1 Zoll Außengewinde AG`
- `GEKA Adapter 1 Zoll AG Messing`
- `GEKA Schlauchkupplung 25mm Anschluss 1 Zoll`

**360° rotatable at the thread — for preventing hose twist (add to fixed brass parts):**

The thread swivels independently of the claw body so the hose can rotate freely. For G1 female (IG / Innengewinde) to mate onto your existing male (AG) thread, article `07.1113`:

- `GEKA plus 360° Gewindestück IG 1 Zoll`
- `GEKA plus 360° Innengewinde G1`
- `GEKA plus 360 drehbar Gewindestück Innengewinde 1 Zoll`
- `GEKA 360° Kupplung Innengewinde G1 Messing`
- `GEKA drehbare Kupplung 1 Zoll IG Messing`
- `GEKA Gewindestück 360 drehbar 1 Zoll IG`
- `GEKA plus 360° Gewindestück G1 IG 07.1113`

**What to look for:** "GEKA plus 360°" branding, "Gewindestück" (thread piece, not hose piece), "Innengewinde" (female thread) G1, "drehbar" (rotatable), material brass (Messing). The claw side is standard 40 mm GEKA — works with any standard GEKA hose piece. Also available with Außengewinde (AG, art. 07.2113) if you need male thread instead.

## GEKA Hose Piece (counterpart for the hose side)

Mates with any standard GEKA claw (including the Gewindestück above). Has a barbed hose tail (Tülle) on one side and a GEKA claw on the other. For 1" (25 mm) hose:

**Standard (non-rotating):**
- `GEKA Schlauchstück 1 Zoll 25mm`
- `GEKA Schlauchkupplung 25mm 1 Zoll`
- `GEKA Schlauchstück 1" 25mm Messing`
- `GEKA Tülle 25mm Klauenkupplung`

**360° rotatable hose tail — prevents hose twist on the hose side, art. 08.4104:**
- `GEKA plus 360° Schlauchstück 1 Zoll 25mm`
- `GEKA plus 360° Schlauchstück 25mm drehbar`
- `GEKA Schlauchstück 360 drehbar 1" 25mm`
- `GEKA plus 360° Schlauchstück 1 Zoll 08.4104`

**What to look for:** "Schlauchstück" (hose piece) or "Schlauchkupplung" (hose coupling), hose inner diameter 25 mm / 1 Zoll, standard 40 mm GEKA claw. If you already have the 360° Gewindestück at the faucet, a standard (non-rotating) Schlauchstück is sufficient — the rotation is already handled on the fixed side. Only get the 360° Schlauchstück if you also want the hose barb to swivel independently (e.g. for a long hose run that twists when walking around corners).

## GEKA to Click Connector / Gardena-Stecksystem (for connecting Gardena-compatible tools and hoses)

Connects a standard GEKA claw (40 mm) to a Gardena-style push-fit click connector. The click connector side comes in two variants: **female (Steckkupplung / Buchse)** = the coupling that receives a male plug, and **male (Klickstecker / Stecker)** = the plug itself. The female side is what you want if you're terminating a fixed line with a quick-connect socket that any Gardena male plug can click into.

**GEKA claw → female click connector (Steckkupplung / Buchse) — what you asked for:**
- `GEKA Gardena Adapter Steckkupplung Messing`
- `Übergangsstück Klauenkupplung auf Steckkupplung Messing`
- `GEKA auf Gardena Kupplung Messing`
- `Klauenkupplung auf Buchse Gardena Messing`
- `Adapter von Geka Schnellkupplung auf Gardena Steckkupplung`
- `Adapter Klauenkupplung auf Weibchen Gardena`
- `Klauen Schnellkupplung Übergang Steckkupplung Gardena Messing`

**GEKA claw → male click plug (Klickstecker / Stecker):**
- `GEKA Gardena Adapter Stecker Messing`
- `Klauenkupplung mit Klickstecker Messing`
- `GEKA auf Gardena Stecker Übergangsstück`
- `Messing Gartenschlauchkupplung Klickstecker x Schnellkupplung`

**What to look for:** Messing (brass), 40 mm GEKA Klauenabstand. The female click side (Steckkupplung) accepts all standard Gardena-compatible 16.5 mm male plugs. The adapter is typically a single-piece brass forging with a GEKA claw on one end and the Gardena-profile end on the other. Some cheaper versions are two-piece with a rotating collar. NBR or EPDM gasket. Betriebsdruck ≥ 8 bar. Use the female variant when you want to terminate a GEKA-based distribution rail with a universal socket; use the male variant when you want to plug a GEKA line directly into an existing Gardena female coupling.

## Gardena Hose Connector with Aqua Stop (hose barb → female click connector, no GEKA)

Hose barb (Tülle) on one side, Gardena-compatible female click connector (Steckkupplung / Buchse) on the other, with integrated Aqua Stop (Wasserstop) — auto shut-off when disconnected. This is the standard end-of-hose fitting for the Gardena click system.

**Gardena original (Kunststoff):**
- `Gardena Schlauchanschluss mit Steckkupplung und Wasserstop`
- `Gardena Wasserstop Schlauchanschluss`
  - Art. 18213-50: 1/2" (13 mm) / 5/8" (15 mm)
  - Art. 18214-50: 3/4" (19 mm)
  - Art. 02814-20: 3/4" Profi-System

**Brass / compatible (Messing, more durable, fits standard Gardena Stecksystem):**
- `Schlauchanschlussstück mit Wasserstop Messing`
- `Schlauchstück mit Wasserstop Messing Steckkupplung`
- `Messing Schlauchverbinder Wasserstop Gardena kompatibel`
- `Gardena kompatibler Schlauchanschluss Wasserstop Messing`
- `Messing Schlauchstück Wasserstopp Stecksystem`
- `Messing Kupplung auf Gardena System Schlauchanschluss`
- `Schlauchanschluss Messing Klick Steckkupplung Wasserstop`

**What to look for:** "Schlauchanschluss", "Schlauchstück", or "Schlauchanschlussstück" (hose connection piece), "Steckkupplung" or "Buchse" (female click connector), "Wasserstop" or "Aquastop" (auto shut-off). Available in 1/2" (13 mm) and 3/4" (19 mm) hose sizes — 1" (25 mm) is not common for this product type. Messing (brass) versions are more durable than Gardena's original Kunststoff. For a 1" hose, either step down to a 3/4" version or use the two-part approach: 1" GEKA Schlauchstück + GEKA-to-Gardena female adapter (see previous section).

## Pipe Clamps 1" / DN25 (wall-mounting the rail)

- `Rohrschelle 1 Zoll Edelstahl VA 32-35mm`
- `Rohrschelle 33mm Edelstahl V4A`
- `Schraubrohrschelle Edelstahl 1 Zoll M8`
- `Stockschraube M8 x 80 Edelstahl`

**What to look for:** Stainless steel (Edelstahl VA or V4A) with rubber insert. Clamping range 32–36 mm (fits 1" / DN25 nominal pipe with ~33 mm OD). M8 thread nut for hanger bolt. Buy matching M8×80 mm stainless steel hanger bolts (Stockschraube) separately if not included.

**Recommended product:** POPPSTAR 2-pack stainless steel clamps 32–35 mm with M8×82 mm hanger bolts and dowels — ~€8 on Amazon. Or MegaHaustechnik stainless steel VA clamps (screws sold separately).

## Ball Valve — Outlet (3x, IG/IG)

- `Kugelhahn 1 Zoll IG IG Messing Durchgang`
- `Kugelhahn G1 Zoll Innengewinde Innengewinde Messing`
- `Absperrhahn 1 Zoll IG IG Messing`
- `Messing Kugelhahn 1 Zoll DN25 IG IG`

**What to look for:** 1" IG×IG (female both sides) — tee branch (AG) screws directly in on one side, GEKA adapter (AG) on the other. Full-bore (Durchgang / Vollbohrung) to avoid restriction. Brass preferred for outdoor use.

- `Kugelhahn G1 Zoll Außengewinde Außengewinde Messing`
- `Messing Kugelhahn 1 Zoll AG AG Durchgang`

**What to look for:** 1" AG×AG (male both sides) — screws directly between two IG/IG flat-sealing unions. Full-bore (Durchgang / Vollbohrung) to avoid restriction.

## GEKA Y-Splitter (1 input → 2 outputs, for splitting 1" hose to two 3/4" hoses)

GEKA claws are all the same 40 mm standard — the hose size (1", 3/4") lives at the hose tail, not the claw. So any GEKA Y-splitter works: connect your 1" Schlauchstück to the input, and two 3/4" Schlauchstücke to the outputs.

**Without shut-off (simple Y):**
- `GEKA Y-Stück Messing`
- `GEKA plus Y-Abzweigstecker Messing`
- `GEKA 3-Wege Verteiler Messing`
- `GEKA Dreiwegestück Messing`

**With individual shut-off valves (recommended — control each branch independently, art. 416039 / 4019416):**
- `GEKA Y-Verteiler absperrbar Messing`
- `GEKA Zweiwegeventil Kugelabsperrventil Messing`
- `GEKA Dreiwegestück absperrbar Messing`
- `GEKA Y-Stück mit Absperrhahn einzeln`
- `GEKA 2-Wege Verteiler Kugelhahn Messing`

**Note on flow:** Since GEKA claws are all the same size, the splitter itself doesn't restrict flow. The restriction is at the hose tails — going from a 1" hose tail to a 3/4" hose tail is a step down. The absperrbar versions use ball valves (Kugelhähne) so you can fully shut off or partially throttle each branch.

**What to look for:** Messing (brass), einzeln absperrbar (individually shut-off), Kugelhähne (ball valves), 40 mm Klauenabstand.

## Retractable Garden Hose / Schlauchbox (for hand watering)

Product category: wall-mountable hose box with automatic spring retraction. For 3/4" (19 mm) hose:

- `Schlauchbox 3/4 Zoll automatisch`
- `Schlauchaufroller 3/4 Zoll Einzugautomatik`
- `Wandschlauchbox 3/4 Zoll`
- `Aufrollschlauch 3/4 Zoll 19mm`
- `automatischer Schlauchaufroller 3/4 Zoll`
- `Schlauchbox 19 mm Rückzugautomatik`
- `Wandschlauchbox 19 mm 3/4"`

**Common gotcha:** most retractable hose boxes come with 1/2" (13 mm) hose — 3/4" is less common in this format. If you find one with 3/4", it will likely have 1/2" fittings at the connection points (the hose itself may be 3/4" but terminates in a 1/2" GEKA or Gardena connector). Verify the hose inner diameter and the connector type match your setup. If the box has 1/2" connectors, you'd need a 1/2" GEKA Schlauchstück (or adapter) to connect it to your 1" brass distribution rail.

**What to look for:** automatischer Rückzug / Einzugautomatik (automatic retraction), Wandmontage (wall-mount), 3/4 Zoll or 19 mm hose diameter, GEKA-compatible or Gardena connectors. If you already have GEKA infrastructure (your 1" rail with the 360° Gewindestück + standard Schlauchstück), prefer a box with GEKA-compatible connections.
