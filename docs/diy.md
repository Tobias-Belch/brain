## Painting IKEA Furniture

[Video: how to paint ikea furniture! by The Brick House Chronicles](https://youtube.com/shorts/9ZMjwmKRgqA)

1. **Sand** the surface to help the paint adhere.
   - [RAUTNER Schleifpapier 230×280 mm Korn 60–240 (15 Stück) — Hornbach](https://www.hornbach.de/p/rautner-schleifpapier-fuer-handschleifer-schwingschleifer-aluminiumoxid-230-x-280-mm-korn-60-80-120-180-240-ungelocht-15-stueck/10501474/)
   - [Bosch Professional Schleifblatt P120 125 mm Holz (5 Stück) — Hellweg](https://www.hellweg.de/a/bosch-professional-schleifblatt-p120-125-mm-holz-5-stueck-138891)
2. **Wipe down** with a damp cloth to remove dust and debris.
3. **Use a roller** for applying paint to get an even finish.
4. **Apply a primer** (e.g. Dulux) to seal the surface.
   - [HORNBACH Acryl Multigrund weiß 750 ml — Hornbach](https://www.hornbach.de/p/hornbach-acryl-multigrund-weiss-750-ml/5870206/)
   - [Clou G1 Schnellschleif-Grundierung 250 ml — Hellweg](https://www.hellweg.de/a/clou-g1-schnellschleif-grundierung-250-ml-142042)
5. **Let it dry** fully before moving on.
6. **Paint with your colour of choice** — eggshell finish is more durable than matt.
   - [HORNBACH Hybridlack Möbellack seidenmatt RAL 9010 750 ml — Hornbach](https://www.hornbach.de/p/hornbach-buntlack-hybridlack-moebellack-seidenmatt-ral-9010-reinweiss-750-ml/8735729/)
   - [Vincent Weißlack 2in1 RAL 9010 seidenmatt 375 ml — Hellweg](https://www.hellweg.de/a/vincent-weisslack-2in1-ral-9010-reinweiss-seidenmatt-375-ml-110684)
7. **Finish with a polyurethane top coat** for a durable and silky smooth result.
   - [HORNBACH Acryl Klarlack seidenmatt 750 ml — Hornbach](https://www.hornbach.de/p/hornbach-acryl-klarlack-seidenmatt-750-ml/3562832/)
   - [Clou EL Holz-Siegel seidenmatt 750 ml — Hellweg](https://www.hellweg.de/a/clou-el-holz-siegel-seidenmatt-750-ml-142104)
